# Zomato-Data-Analysis-SQL
A comprehensive SQL-based analysis of Zomato’s restaurant data to solve key business problems like rating trends, delivery optimization, and cost segmentation.
# Key Insights & Findings
**City Rating Trends:** Several cities, including Faridabad, Noida, and Gurgaon, show average ratings below the overall platform average. These are key areas for Zomato to implement quality improvement programs.

**Top Pick in Kolkata:** For users like Mr. Roy, "Barbeque Nation" (Restaurant ID: 20870) stands out as the best choice, holding the top rank for online delivery with a high rating and significant vote count.

**Pizza in New Delhi:** Peter’s best option for Pizza in New Delhi is "Saravana Bhavan", which remarkably holds the highest rating in that category.

**Global Footprint:** Zomato's largest presence is overwhelmingly in India, followed by the United States and the United Arab Emirates.

**Cost vs. Luxury:** The Average Cost for Two across all restaurants is approximately 1200 units (local currency).
Most restaurants (95%+) fall into the "Average" cost category. Truly "Luxurious Expensive" venues make up less than 1% of the total listings, highlighting Zomato's strength in the mass market.

**Offline Service Gaps:** Several restaurants were flagged for poor offline ratings despite high online popularity, suggesting a need to improve the in-person dining experience.
